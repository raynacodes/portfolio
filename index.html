about

Hello! My name is Rayna Butler and I am a self-taught web developer, specializing in HTML Email Development.

A bit about me... In 2019 I went to college in NYC and was pursuing a career in the fashion business industry, but rerouted my career pursuits when I discoverd the world of coding. I instantly fell in love with the combination of technicality and problem-solving, paired with design, layout, and strategy.

I think this is why I have gravitated towards Email Development. It goes beyond just coding, and expands into branding, marketing, and having an online presence.

My goal in web development is to make work that is simple and clean, yet stands out among the rest of the world wide web.
